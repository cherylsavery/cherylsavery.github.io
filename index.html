The goal of Mind Active is to develop games that are accessible to everyone. Mind Active games will focus on fun as well as thinking and planning as opposed to timing and quick reflexes.

Coffee Crash

Swipe to move water drops and coffee beans to make cups of coffee. Making coffee is easy, but careful planning is required to not lose your beans and water and to make groups of 4 or 5 coffees.

        

CodeWord

A challenging crossword-like puzzle where numbers represent each letter

   

CodeWord Pro: All puzzles are unlocked. No in-app purchases. Pay once - play forever.

     

CodeWord Free: Start with 5 puzzles. Watch videos to access additional puzzles. Includes all the features of CodeWord Pro.

     

CodeWord Canada

Celebrate everything Canadian with 100 puzzles containing unique Canadian words, places and themes.

   

     

Circle Smash

This is not a typical "Mind Active" game. I made it for my granddaughter who wanted something with a bit more action in it.

Tap the circles to make them disappear, but don't tap too many or you won't score any points. A good fun family-friendly game.
