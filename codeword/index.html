Description

Challenging and addictive - CodeWord puzzles are like crossword puzzles, except there are no clues. Every square in the puzzle contains a number that matches a letter in the alphabet. Solve the puzzle by finding the letter for each number.

100 beautifully designed puzzles to entertain and challenge you. 5 levels of difficulty from beginner to extreme.

Every puzzle contains all 26 letters of the alphabet and has been handcrafted to ensure the greatest possible variety of familiar, yet challenging words.

How To Play

Chose a cell in the codeword grid and then choose the letter you think may fit.

As you fill in more and more letters in the puzzle, words begin to appear making it easier to determine the next letter.

If you are stuck, use the hint button to add a letter to the puzzle or use the check button to see if you have any errors.
