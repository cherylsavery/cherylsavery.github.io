About Mind Active

Mind Active is a maker of mobile games that are accessible to everyone. The games generally focus on fun as well as thinking and planning, as opposed to timing and quick reflexes.

In Coffee Crash, you swipe to move water drops and coffee beans to make cups of coffee. There is no time pressure to make your next move and careful planning is required to not lose your beans and water and to make groups of 4 or 5 coffees.

In CodeWord, although the puzzles are timed, there is no requirement to solve the puzzle quickly. Thus, each player can proceed at their own pace. The multiple difficulty levels and the hint button allow players to choose how hard they want to work to solve the puzzle.

Developer

Mind Active games have been developed by Cheryl Savery.

I graduated in 2014 from Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario, Canada with a PhD in Computing. My research focused on consistency maintenance in networked games and I developed the Janus Toolkit that simplifies the implementation of a variety of game networking techniques.
