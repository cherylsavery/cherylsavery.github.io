Description

Coffee Crash is a casual game in which players combine water and coffee beans to make cups of coffee. The objective is to make groups of three coffees and score points. The game is played on a simple 5 by 5 grid. Swiping left or right or up or down moves all the objects in the grid. When a coffee bean crashes into a water drop, it changes into a cup of coffee.

Obstacles such as walls that block moves and holes that swallow up coffee cups and coffee beans add to the challenge of the game.

Coffee perks such as wood to block the holes and stars that match any object allow the player to overcome the obstacles and score more points.

How To Play

The only thing you need to do to play Coffee Crash is swipe. If you swipe left, all the objects in the grid will move left one space, unless they bump into another object or bump into the edge of the grid. Objects will never fall off the edge of the grid.

You can swipe any direction. If you swipe up, the objects move up. If you swipe down, the objects move down, etc.

Some objects can be combined. At the start of the game, water dropsand coffee beans can be combined by moving one of them on top of the other. When they combine, a cup of coffee is formed.

Whenever you make 3 objects in a row, they will disappear and you will score points. You will score more points if you can get 4 in a row.

You have a limited number of turns to meet the target score. The donuts at the top of the grid show you how many turns you have left. 1 donut represents 10 moves.

Playing Tips

  • Try not to make 3 coffee beans or water drops in a row. If you do, you lose them and will not be able to use them to make coffee cups. The beans and water score much less than the coffee.
  • Try to set up 4 or 5 coffee cups in a row. You get bonus points when you do this.
  • If you are having trouble making 4 or 5 in a row, just try avoiding making 3 in a row. After a while you may have so many cups that a group of 4 just happens
  • Pay attention to the Next object that will be added to the grid. It always shows up somewhere on the side that you swiped from. For example, if you swiped down, the next object will appear in the top row. Try to add objects near the ones it will combine with. If all your beans are near the bottom and the next object is a water, swipe up to get a water at the bottom.
  • If you are really stuck on a level use one or more of the coffee perks to help you pass it.

Coffee Perks

Starting at level 9, you can use one or more Coffee Perks to help you pass the level. You start the game with 3 of each type of perk.

You can earn more perks in the Coffee Perk grid which becomes available after you complete level 9. If you find a match, you earn an additional perk. After you try for a match, you must play the main Coffee Crash game 3 times before you can try again.
